DELETE DeleteHealthCheck

newtype DeleteHealthCheck

Deletes a health check.

Caution: Route 53 does not prevent you from deleting a health check even if the health check is associated with one or more resource record sets. If you delete a health check and you don't update the associated resource record sets, the future status of the health check cannot be predicted and may change. This will affect the routing of DNS queries for your DNS failover configuration.

http://docs.aws.amazon.com/Route53/latest/APIReference/API_DeleteHealthCheck.html
